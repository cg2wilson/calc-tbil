Definition 11.2.3. Polar Double Integral (General Region).

The double integral over a polar region \(D\), where
\begin{equation*} D = \lrbrace{(r,\theta)\mid \alpha\leq\theta\leq\beta, h_1(\theta)\leq r\leq h_2(\theta)} \end{equation*}
is given by
\begin{equation*} \ds \iint_D f(r,\theta) \,r\,dr\,d\theta = \int_{\theta = \alpha}^{\theta=\beta} \int_{r=h_1(\theta)}^{r=h_2(\theta)} f(r,\theta)\,r\,dr\,d\theta \end{equation*}
