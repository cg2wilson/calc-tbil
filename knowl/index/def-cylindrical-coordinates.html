Definition 11.4.1. Cylindrical Coordinates.

The cylindrical coordinate system is one where the coordinates of the object in question are projected onto a cylinder. The conversion factors are
\begin{equation*} x = r\cos\theta \qquad y = r\sin\theta \qquad z = z \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = r^2 \end{equation*}
