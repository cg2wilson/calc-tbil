Theorem 12.1.4. Area Under a Parametric Curve.

Let \(x=x(t)\) and \(y = y(t)\) be a non-self-intersecting plane curve on the interval \(a\leq t\leq b\text{;}\) assume that \(x(t)\) is differentiable. Then, the area under the curve is given by
\begin{equation*} A = \int_a^b y(t) x'(t)\, dt \end{equation*}
