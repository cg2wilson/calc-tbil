Theorem 13.4.3. The Divergence Theorem.

Let \(S\) be a piecewise, smooth, closed surface that encloses a solid \(E\) in space. Assume that \(S\) is oriented outward, and let \(\textbf{F}\) be a vector field with continuous partial derivatives on an open region containing \(E\text{.}\) Then,
\begin{equation*} \iiint_E \div{\textbf{F}}\,dV = \iint_S \textbf{F}\cdot d\textbf{S} \end{equation*}
