Theorem 8.6.1. The Ratio Test.

For the series \(\ds \sum a_n\text{,}\) let
\begin{equation*} \ds \lim_{n\to\infty} \left|\dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| = L \end{equation*}
Then, if
  • \(L \lt 1\text{,}\) the series converges absolutely
  • \(L \gt 1\text{,}\) the series diverges
  • \(L = 1\text{,}\) the ratio test is inconclusive and another test must be used
