Definition 13.2.4. Surface Area.

For smooth parametric surface \(S\) given by \(\textbf{r}(u,v) = \lrangle{f(t),g(t),h(t)}\text{,}\) \(u,v\in D\text{,}\) such that \(S\) is covered once by \(\textbf{r}\text{,}\) then the surface area of \(S\) is given by
\begin{equation*} A(S) = \iint_D |\textbf{r}_u\times\textbf{r}_v|\, dA \end{equation*}
