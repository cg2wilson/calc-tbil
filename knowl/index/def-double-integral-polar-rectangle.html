Definition 11.2.2. Polar Double Integral (Rectangular Region).

The double integral over a polar rectangle \(R\) of the function \(f(r,\theta)\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \iint_R f(r,\theta)\, dA = \iint_R f(r,\theta)\,r\,dr\,d\theta = \int_{\theta=\alpha}^{\theta=\beta}\int_{r=a}^{r=b}f(r,\theta)\,r\,dr\,d\theta \end{equation*}
