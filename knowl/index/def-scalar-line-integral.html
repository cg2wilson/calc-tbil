Definition 12.3.5. Line Integral (Scalar).

Let \(f\) be a continuous function with a domain that includes the smooth curve \(C\) with parametrization \(\textbf{r}(t)\) on \(a\leq t\leq b\text{.}\) Then, the scalar line integral of \(f\) over \(C\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \int_C f\, ds = \int_a^b f(\textbf{r}(t))||\textbf{r}'(t)||\, dt \end{equation*}
