Theorem 12.1.5. Arc Length of a Parametric Curve.

Consider the plane curve defined by the parametric equations \(x = x(t)\text{,}\) \(y = y(t)\) on the interval \(t_1 \leq t\leq t_2\text{,}\) and assume that both functions are differentiable. Then, the arc length of the curve is given by
\begin{equation*} s = \int_{t_1}^{t_2} \sqrt{\lrpar{\dfrac{dx}{dt}}^2 + \lrpar{\dfrac{dy}{dt}}^2} \end{equation*}
