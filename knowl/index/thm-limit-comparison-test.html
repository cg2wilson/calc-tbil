Theorem 8.4.1. The Limit Comparison Test.

Let \(\ds\sum a_n\) and \(\ds\sum b_n\) be series with positive terms. If
\begin{equation*} \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{b_n}{a_n} = c \end{equation*}
for some positive (finite) constant \(c\text{,}\) then \(\ds\sum a_n\) and \(\ds\sum b_n\) either both converge or both diverge.
